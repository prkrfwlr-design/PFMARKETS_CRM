Fair questions, straight answers

Do I need to film myself?

No. If you've got a phone and a crew willing to grab clips on a job, that's plenty. I'll tell you exactly what to shoot. If not, I can source UGC-style creators to build footage from scratch.

How many ads do I get per month?

Depends on the plan, but the baseline is a fresh batch of creative every 2 to 3 weeks, enough to stay ahead of ad fatigue without paying for a new shoot every week.

What if a piece of creative flops?

It happens, not every concept lands. I watch performance and kill or replace anything underperforming instead of leaving it to burn budget.

Stock photos vs. custom footage, does it matter that much?

For exterior cleaning, yes. People are hiring you to show up at their house. Real footage of real work builds more trust in three seconds than a polished stock photo ever will.

Can you do creative without also running our ads?

Yes, plenty of clients run their own media buying and just need the creative pipeline. I'll hand off finished crops ready to upload.
