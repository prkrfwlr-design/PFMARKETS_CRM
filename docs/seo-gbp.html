Fair questions, straight answers

How long until I see ranking movement?

Most businesses see meaningful map-pack movement around 3 to 6 months in, depending on how competitive your market is. Anyone promising page-one rankings in two weeks is either lying or gaming something that'll get you penalized.

Do I still need ads while this builds?

Usually, yes, at least at the start. SEO and ads solve different timelines. I'm upfront that this isn't a replacement for paid leads in month one.

What's the review generation system, exactly?

A simple text or email flow triggered after a job wraps, asking the customer for a review with a direct link. No gimmicks, no buying reviews, just making it easy for happy customers to actually leave one.

Will this work in a competitive market?

It'll help in any market, but a saturated one takes longer and more consistent work. I'll tell you honestly where you stand after the audit instead of promising a number.

What if my GBP is already suspended or restricted?

I've dealt with it before. It's a slower fix, a reinstatement request plus cleanup, but it's usually fixable. I'll tell you the real odds before you pay for anything.
