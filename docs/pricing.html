Pricing questions

Why is The Works cheaper than buying two services separately?
Because everything works better together and costs me less to run as one system. Ads plus SEO plus email on the same account share creative, data, and setup. I pass that efficiency on, and clients on The Works stay longer, which is worth more to me than the margin.
What's the CRM and do I need it?
It's your client portal: every lead, job, and report in one place instead of scattered texts and spreadsheets. It's $29 a month as an add-on, included free in The Works. You don't need it to work with me, but most clients end up living in it.
Am I locked into anything?
No. Everything is month-to-month, including The Works. The $297 website is yours, you own it either way.
How does the free plus-one actually work?
Book any one paid service and pick a second one free: a month of ad management, a GBP optimization, or a website. If the freebie earns its keep, you can keep it at the normal rate. If not, drop it, no hard feelings.
Does ad spend come out of the management fee?
No, ad spend goes straight to Meta from your card and you see it. My fee covers strategy, creative, building, and managing. Most of my clients run $500 to $2,000 a month in spend, and I'll tell you honestly what your market needs.
