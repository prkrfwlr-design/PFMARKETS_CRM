Parker, Anderson, SC

PFMarkets grew out of PFCleans, my exterior cleaning company here in Anderson, South Carolina. Before I had a client roster, I had a schedule to fill, a crew to pay, and a phone that either rang or didn't.

I built the ads, the Google Business Profile, and the website to keep PFCleans booked. When it worked, other owners in the same trades started asking who was running it. That's the whole origin story. Nothing sold, nothing pitched. A system that had to work because I had no fallback plan if it didn't.

I still run every account personally. There's no account manager standing between you and the person who built your campaign, and no junior hire learning your business on your dime.

Why only exterior cleaning

Generalist agencies chase every industry that'll sign a contract. I don't, on purpose.

I know the unit economics

Ticket size, route density, crew hours, slow season. I don't have to ask what these mean for your business because I've run the same numbers on my own.

The playbook actually repeats

A driveway wash lead and a storefront glass route behave differently online, but the fundamentals repeat: local intent, fast response, before/after proof. Depth beats breadth here.

I can say no to bad fits

If a business outside exterior cleaning calls, I'm honest that it's not my lane. Staying narrow means every client gets someone who's actually seen their problem before, not a generalist guessing.

What I refuse to do

A short list, kept short on purpose. These aren't marketing promises, they're operating rules.

  • Lock-in contracts

    Everything here is month-to-month. If I'm not earning the invoice, you shouldn't need a lawyer to leave.

  • Vanity metrics

    I won't hand you a report full of impressions and reach and call it a win. Booked jobs are the only number that pays your crew.

  • Set-it-and-forget-it campaigns

    Ads that ran the same way for six months are ads nobody's watching. I tune weekly, especially in the first 90 days.

  • Ticket-system silence

    No support portal, no "submit a request" black hole. You text me and I answer, because I already know your account.
